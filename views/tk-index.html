Thank You, Zach Latta!

We would like to extend our heartfelt gratitude to Zach Latta for his incredible contributions to the tech community. His dedication to empowering young people through technology and coding has inspired countless individuals around the world.

Zach's work with Hack Club has created a supportive environment for students to learn, create, and innovate. His passion for education and technology has made a significant impact, and we are truly thankful for his efforts.

Thank you, Zach, for being a beacon of inspiration and for all that you do to make the world a better place through technology!
